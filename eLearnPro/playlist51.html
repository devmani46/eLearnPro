Machine learning for beginners

Machine learning is a branch of artificial intelligence (AI) that focuses on the development of algorithms and models that enable computer systems to learn and make predictions or decisions without explicit programming. It involves the use of statistical techniques and algorithms to train computers on large amounts of data, allowing them to recognize patterns, make predictions, or take actions based on that data.
